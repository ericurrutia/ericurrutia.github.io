WHO I AM

 

What's up. I'm Eric. Intros are always fun because I give others a glimpse into my life. I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I think this is where I had the opportunity to be artistic without anyone judging.


I go to the University of California, Berkeley. I really enjoy music and film. I'm a filmer. I've been influenced by many events in my life. I'm very free-spirited and open to new experiences. I'm very creative and I am looking to expand my work and reach the extraordinary.
